Email Drafting

Drafts replies in your voice. You review and send. Iris learns your tone from your writing history and matches it exactly — professional, warm, or terse, depending on context.

🔔

Follow-up Tracking

Never let a deal, intro, or task fall through. Iris tracks every open thread and nudges you when something needs a response — before the other person follows up first.

📋

Meeting Prep

Before every meeting, Iris sends a 1-page brief: who you're meeting, their background, last interaction, open items, and 3 talking points. Shows up prepared, every time.

🎯

Priority Filtering

Distinguishes signal from noise. Routes low-priority items to a digest. Surfaces only the 3–5 things that genuinely need your attention today — nothing more.
